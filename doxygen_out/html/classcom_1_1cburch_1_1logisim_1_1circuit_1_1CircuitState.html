Detailed Description

CircuitState holds the simulation state of a Circuit (or Subcircuit), i.e. the values being carried along all wires and buses, along with the InstanceData for all components embedded in the circuit. Most of the dynamically-computed data is actually in CircuitWires. In here there is mostly just a few pointers to other data structures and the dirty lists (lists of locations or components that need to be recomputed).

Note: Each CircuitState belongs to (at most) one Propagator. Some of the members in here more properly belong to Propagator (or, vice versa, some of the functionality in Propagator could equally well be in here.
