static int com.cburch.logisim.std.io.HexDigit.getSegs ( int  value)
inlinestatic

Maps integer value to display segments. Each nibble is one segment, in top-down, left-to-right order, middle three nibbles are the three horizontal segments:

··A·· F···B ··G·· E···C ··D··

bits: 0xFEAGDBC i.e.: 0x1110111 => "0" shape
